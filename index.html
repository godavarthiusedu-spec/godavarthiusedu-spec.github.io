Artifact 1 — AI Lab

A live AI product I designed, tested, and deployed

Compliance & Trade-Monitoring Assistant

Generative AI Chatbase Design Thinking Responsible AI
Objective
Design and deploy a knowledge-grounded AI assistant that helps compliance analysts and new team members understand trade-monitoring concepts in plain language — while safely refusing legal rulings, investment advice, and requests for live data.
Process
I used a design-thinking approach: identified the audience and their pain points, defined the problem, and prototyped a chatbot on Chatbase. I authored a generic, non-confidential knowledge base and a custom system prompt encoding the assistant's role, knowledge boundaries, guardrails, and response style. I then tested it with six prompts — including three deliberate “refusal” tests — and confirmed each passed before publishing it as a public page.
Tools
Chatbase (no-code AI-agent platform, GPT-based model); a large language model for drafting and refining content; Microsoft Word for the documentation.
Value
Shows I can build, test, and deploy a real AI product end-to-end — and that I design for safety, not just capability. Building an assistant that helpfully refuses out-of-scope requests demonstrates exactly the judgment employers want in AI/ML professionals.
